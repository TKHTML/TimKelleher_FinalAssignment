The whole word is a stage

Stage.js is a jquery plug-in that allows for user-configurable, dynamic, and constant background for your websites and landing pages.

Pulling from stock photos, fair use art off dribbble and deviantart, our advanced algorithm based AI picks the best match based on your needs and your style.

With our convenient, auto-generating background image resource, you no longer need to worry about image quality, artifacting, or wasting server space hosting background images - your work can easily be streamlined and your life can be made much, much easier.

How does it work?

Simply give the plug-in a hex code, a buzz word to search, and whether you want it to match up or act as a complimentary tone to your current schema. Just let stage do the work, and it'll properly frame your backgrounds elegantly

pulling from a pre-configured database, Stage contains an AI that searches, grabs, uploads, and streams images for you - meaning you don't have to do a single bit of work

How the structure works

  • Stage - pulling from the Stage.js script tag.
  • Color - Self explanatory, a standard hex code.
  • frame - where the background will go.
  • subject - what kind of background to search for.
  • style - whether you want it to match the specific hex code you wrote in, or you would rather it be a complimentary color to the hex you put in.
  • width/height - standard measurements to ensure your image doesn't appear too small on your page.
